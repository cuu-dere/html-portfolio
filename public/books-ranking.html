The Best Books According to Anna

My top 3 books of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

Ghibli's movie was amazing, and the book is even better. I love how D.W. Jones mixed fantasy and real world together.

Sleeping Beauties

I don't usually read horrors or thrillers, but I accidentally stumbled upon it after publication, and I don't regret it. Didn't have nightmares after that, but really made me think "what would happen, if..."

Norwegian Wood

I like how Murkami weaves the heavy topis, so they're rather light to read.
